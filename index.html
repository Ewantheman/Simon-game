Press A Key to Start

1. Press a key to start.
2. Simon will give the first signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color lens. This brings u to next level.
3. At next level, Simon will add one additional signal. Repeat these two signals by pressing the same color lenses, in order.
4. Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
5. If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, Simon responds with a"RAZZ" sound. This means you've lost, and the sequence of signals ends.
